AC AMC vs One-Time Service:
Which Saves More Money in Delhi NCR?

By Air Control Experts · April 2026 · 8 min read

Every summer, Delhi NCR homeowners face the same dilemma: book a one-time AC service before the heat peaks, or invest in an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) that covers the whole year? With temperatures regularly crossing 45°C in May and June, your AC isn't a luxury — it's a lifeline. Choosing the wrong service model can cost you thousands in avoidable repairs.

After 38 years servicing ACs across Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida and Faridabad, Air Control has seen both approaches succeed and fail. Here's an honest, data-backed comparison to help you decide.

What Is an AC AMC?

An AC Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a fixed-price annual agreement where a certified technician visits your home on a scheduled basis — typically 2 to 4 times per year — to inspect, clean, and service your AC. Most AMCs also include priority response for breakdowns and discounts on spare parts.

Air Control AMC includes: Pre-summer service visit, mid-season check, filter & coil deep clean, refrigerant pressure check, electrical inspection, drain cleaning, and priority booking for breakdowns. Basic plan: ₹1,499/AC/year. Comprehensive (with parts): ₹2,499/AC/year.

What Is a One-Time AC Service?

A one-time service means you call a technician on-demand whenever your AC needs attention — usually before summer, after a breakdown, or when performance drops. You pay a fixed fee per visit, typically ₹499–₹799 for a standard service, plus extra for parts and gas top-up if needed.

When Does AMC Make Financial Sense?

1. You Have 2 or More ACs

With a multi-unit AMC, the per-AC cost drops significantly. If you have 3 ACs on a basic AMC at ₹1,299/unit, you're spending ₹3,897 for 6 service visits plus priority breakdown cover — far cheaper than 6 individual calls at ₹600 each (₹3,600) with no priority benefit.

2. Your AC Runs 8+ Hours Daily

In Delhi, most households run their ACs from April through October — that's roughly 7 months of heavy operation. High-usage ACs accumulate dust in filters and coils faster, causing efficiency loss and compressor strain. Regular AMC visits catch these issues before they become expensive breakdowns.

3. You Can't Afford a Surprise Summer Breakdown

In peak summer (May–June), technicians are booked 3–5 days out. AMC customers get priority slots. If your AC breaks on a 46°C afternoon, that priority is worth the entire annual fee by itself.

4. Your AC Is Older Than 4 Years

Older ACs need more attention — capacitor wear, refrigerant micro-leaks, worn fan bearings. A comprehensive AMC with parts coverage can save ₹3,000–₹8,000 compared to reactive repair costs on aging units.

When One-Time Service Is the Right Choice

1. You Have a New AC (Under Warranty)

If your AC is under manufacturer warranty, a one-time pre-summer service is sufficient. The manufacturer covers parts, and new units rarely need mid-season attention.

2. You Use the AC Occasionally

If your AC runs less than 4 hours per day or only during weekends, the dust and wear accumulation is low enough that a single annual service adequately maintains efficiency.

3. You're Renting or Moving Soon

If you're renting and unsure whether you'll be in the same flat next summer, locking into an AMC doesn't make sense. A one-time service when needed is more practical.

AMC Coverage: What to Check Before Signing

  • Does it include gas top-up / refrigerant refill, or is that charged extra?
  • Are spare parts covered, or only labour?
  • How many service visits are scheduled — 2 or 4?
  • What is the response time SLA for breakdowns?
  • Does it cover all brands (Daikin, Voltas, LG, Samsung, Carrier, Blue Star)?

Air Control AMC covers all major brands and models — split, window, cassette, and floor-standing ACs. We service Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, Greater Noida, and surrounding NCR areas.

Verdict: AMC or One-Time?

Choose AMC if: you have 2+ ACs, your units are over 3 years old, you use AC heavily April–October, or you can't afford a summer breakdown delay.

Choose one-time service if: your AC is new and under warranty, you use it occasionally, or you're in a temporary rental.

For most Delhi NCR families running 2 or more ACs through a brutal summer, an AMC pays for itself with the first avoided emergency call.
