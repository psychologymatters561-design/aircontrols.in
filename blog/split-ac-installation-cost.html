What Drives Costs Up?

Long Pipe Runs

Every installation comes with 3 metres of copper piping. If the indoor unit is far from the outdoor unit (common in DDA flats where the outdoor must go on the terrace), you'll need extra pipe at ₹600–₹800/metre. A 10-metre run adds ₹4,200–₹5,600 to the base cost.

High-Floor Installations

Installing an outdoor unit on the 6th floor or above requires additional safety rigging and time. Expect a surcharge of ₹500–₹1,500 depending on the building type.

Wall Construction Type

Standard brick walls are easy to drill through. RCC (reinforced cement concrete) walls, common in modern Gurgaon and Noida apartment buildings, require a hammer drill with masonry bits and take longer — adding ₹300–₹600 to the job.

No Existing Power Point

AC units need a dedicated 15-ampere socket. If your room doesn't have one near the installation site, an electrician must run a new circuit — typically ₹800–₹2,000 depending on cable length and panel distance.

Watch out for: Installers quoting ₹800–₹999 "all-inclusive" online. When they arrive, every component — bracket, pipe, drain, wire — is charged separately, and the final bill hits ₹4,000+. Always ask for an itemised quote before confirming.

What a Good Installation Includes

  • Indoor unit mounted at correct height (7–8 feet), level and secure
  • Outdoor unit on heavy-duty MS bracket, properly torqued bolts
  • Copper piping insulated with foam sleeve (prevents sweating)
  • All pipe joints brazed — not just twisted or taped
  • Drain pipe routed to proper discharge point, not onto a neighbour's AC
  • Vacuum purge of the refrigerant circuit before gas fill
  • Test run verifying cooling and checking for leaks

Post-Installation Check: 3 Things to Verify

  1. Indoor unit slope: The indoor unit should tilt very slightly downward toward the drain side. If it's level or backward, condensation will drip into the room.
  2. Pipe insulation: Every metre of copper pipe should be covered in foam insulation tape — bare pipe sweats in Delhi humidity and wastes energy.
  3. Gas pressure: Ask the technician to show you the manifold gauge reading after installation. A 1.5-ton AC should show 60–70 PSI on the low side at ambient temperature.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does split AC installation cost in Delhi NCR?

Standard split AC installation in Delhi NCR costs ₹1,200–₹1,800 for labour. Add ₹600–₹1,200 for copper piping beyond 3m, ₹400–₹600 for outdoor bracket, and ₹300–₹500 for drain pipe. Typical total: ₹2,500–₹4,000.

Is manufacturer installation cheaper than third-party?

Manufacturer installation typically costs ₹3,500–₹6,000. Third-party installers like Air Control charge ₹2,500–₹4,000 with warranty on installation workmanship and the same trained technicians.

What is included in AC installation?

Standard installation includes: indoor unit wall mounting, outdoor unit bracket mounting, 3 metres copper piping, drain pipe connection, electrical connection to existing socket, gas pressure check, and test run.

How long does split AC installation take?

A standard installation takes 2–3 hours. Complex installations involving wall drilling through thick concrete, long pipe runs above 6 metres, or high-rise buildings can take 4–5 hours.

Do I need a stabilizer for a 5-star AC in Delhi?

Modern inverter ACs (2020+) have built-in voltage protection up to 290V. In areas with frequent voltage spikes (many Delhi colonies), a stabilizer adds protection for ₹1,500–₹3,000.
