Questions, Answered

What facilities managers
and homeowners ask us.

How much does an AC maintenance contract (AMC) cost in Delhi NCR?
We price every AMC after a site assessment, because a three-AC home and a 40-unit facility are different commitments. Home and small-office plans start from ₹208 per month. Commercial and institutional contracts — multi-unit offices, embassies, healthcare facilities, cold-chain and industrial sites — are quoted bespoke after a formal commercial assessment. No fixed menu, no obligation to proceed.
What does an Air Control maintenance contract include?
Every engagement runs a full 9-point protocol: evaporator and condenser coils cleaned, filter serviced, condensate drain flushed, electrical connections checked, capacitor health recorded, refrigerant pressure measured against specification, fan motor and blades inspected, and thermostat calibrated. AMC clients receive a written condition report every six months and priority emergency response year-round.
How fast do you respond to a breakdown?
AMC clients are never placed in an open queue. Commercial and institutional sites receive a 2-hour response; residential clients a 4-hour response — a team dispatched, not a promise to call back. We operate 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week, every day of the year.
Do you handle large commercial and institutional HVAC maintenance?
Yes. Since 1987 we have maintained HVAC systems for diplomatic missions and embassies, national industry and manufacturing groups, healthcare environments, logistics and cold-chain facilities, and retail and hospitality chains across Delhi NCR — including multi-unit and multi-campus contracts. The same protocol and documentation apply at every scale.
Which areas of Delhi NCR do you serve?
We serve the entire Delhi NCR region, including South Delhi, Defence Colony, Greater Kailash, East of Kailash, Connaught Place and central Delhi, plus Gurugram, Noida, Ghaziabad and Faridabad. We are based in East of Kailash, South Delhi.
Do you service all AC brands and system types?
Yes. We service all major air-conditioning brands and every common system type — split, window, cassette, ducted and VRV/VRF systems — across residential, commercial and industrial installations.
What is a commercial assessment, and is it free?
A commercial assessment is a written, unit-by-unit technical evaluation of a facility's air-conditioning: condition ratings, efficiency readings, component health, estimated remaining life and a prioritised action list. It is a technical document, not a sales visit. It carries a fee, credited against your AMC if you sign within 30 days. Home and small-office assessments are complimentary.
Why is preventive maintenance worth it in Delhi?
In Delhi's climate, most summer breakdowns are decided months earlier — by coils nobody cleaned and components nobody checked. A single unplanned breakdown in peak summer typically costs more than a full year of preventive maintenance, and arrives at the worst possible time. Scheduled maintenance converts an unpredictable emergency into a fixed, planned cost.
