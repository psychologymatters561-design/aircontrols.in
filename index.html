AC Repair, AC Servicing & HVAC Services – Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best AC repair services in Delhi NCR?

We provide professional AC repair services in Delhi NCR including split AC repair, central air conditioning repair, VRF system repair, and industrial HVAC troubleshooting with fast response.

How often should AC servicing be done?

AC servicing should be done every 3 to 6 months depending on usage. Regular air conditioner servicing improves cooling efficiency, reduces electricity bills, and prevents breakdowns.

What is included in AC servicing?

AC servicing includes cleaning filters, checking refrigerant gas levels, inspecting electrical components, condenser cleaning, and ensuring proper airflow for optimal cooling.

What is AC AMC and why is it important?

AC AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) ensures regular AC maintenance, reduces repair costs, and extends the life of air conditioning systems for residential and commercial use.

What is the cost of AC servicing in Delhi NCR?

The cost of AC servicing depends on system type such as split AC, window AC, or central air conditioning systems. Commercial HVAC AMC pricing varies based on system size.

Do you provide AC installation services?

Yes, we provide air conditioner installation services including split AC installation, ducted AC systems, VRF systems, and central AC installation across Delhi NCR.

Which AC is best for home use?

Split AC and inverter AC systems are ideal for homes due to energy efficiency and better cooling performance. The right air conditioner depends on room size and usage.

What is the difference between HVAC and air conditioning?

Air conditioning focuses on cooling, while HVAC includes heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems used for residential, commercial, and industrial environments.

Do you provide AC repair near me?

Yes, we provide AC repair services near you across Delhi NCR including Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad with fast response.

How can I reduce AC electricity bills?

Regular AC servicing, proper installation, using inverter AC systems, and maintaining optimal temperature settings can significantly reduce electricity consumption.

What types of air conditioners do you service?

We service split AC, window AC, cassette AC, ducted air conditioning, VRF systems, VRV systems, central AC, and chiller-based HVAC systems.

Do you provide commercial HVAC services?

Yes, we provide commercial HVAC installation, AC maintenance, and HVAC AMC services for offices, factories, hospitals, hotels, and industrial facilities.

What is the best AC maintenance plan?

The best AC maintenance plan is an AMC contract that includes periodic servicing, inspection, and preventive maintenance to avoid major breakdowns.

Do you offer emergency AC repair services?

Yes, we provide emergency AC repair and HVAC services with quick response across Delhi NCR for residential and commercial systems.

Which areas do you cover for AC services?

We provide AC servicing, AC repair, AC installation, and HVAC services in Saket, Lajpat Nagar, Okhla, Rohini, Janakpuri, Connaught Place, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad.

Why is my AC not cooling even after servicing?

If your AC is not cooling after servicing, it could be due to a gas (refrigerant) leak, faulty compressor, blocked expansion valve, or undersized AC for the room. Our engineers diagnose the root cause and fix it permanently. We cover all brands including Daikin, Voltas, LG, Blue Star, Hitachi, Samsung, and Carrier.

How much does AC gas filling cost in Delhi?

AC gas filling (refrigerant top-up) in Delhi typically costs ₹1,200–₹3,500 depending on the gas type (R-22, R-32, R-410A) and system capacity. We use only certified, non-flammable refrigerants and always check for leaks before refilling to ensure lasting results.

What is the difference between AC servicing and AC repair?

AC servicing is preventive maintenance — cleaning filters, washing the condenser, checking gas pressure and electrical connections to keep your air conditioner running efficiently. AC repair is corrective — fixing a broken part, resolving no-cooling issues, or replacing a faulty compressor or PCB. Both are available across Delhi NCR from Air Control.

Which AC brands do you service and repair in Delhi?

We service and repair all major AC brands in Delhi including Daikin, Voltas, Blue Star, Hitachi, LG, Samsung, Carrier, O General, Mitsubishi, Panasonic, Godrej, Lloyd, Whirlpool, and Haier — for both residential and commercial air conditioning systems.
